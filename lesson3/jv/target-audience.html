    Who We Serve

    Our chamber of commerce serves a diverse group of businesses in the Point of the Mountain area, including:

    • Small businesses
    • Large corporations
    • Non-profit organizations
    • Entrepreneurs

    Personas

    Here are two examples of people who might be interested in our chamber:

    • Name: Sarah
    • Occupation: Owner of a local bakery
    • Age: 32
    • Goals: Increase sales, connect with other business owners in the community
    • Name: John
    • Occupation: CEO of a technology company
    • Age: 45
    • Goals: Find qualified employees, expand business operations

    Scenarios

    1. A small business owner wants to attend a networking event to meet other business owners in the area.
    2. A non-profit organization needs help with fundraising and wants to find out if the chamber can provide resources.
    3. A large corporation is planning to open a new branch in the Point of the Mountain area and needs information about local taxes and regulations.
    4. An entrepreneur is looking for a mentor to help them start their own business.
    5. A business owner is interested in sponsoring a community event and wants to know how to get involved.
