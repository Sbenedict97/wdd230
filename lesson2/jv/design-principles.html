Visual Hierarchy

Organization/Company Name: Apple

Website Address: https://www.apple.com

The use of contrasting colors and large headings on the Apple website create a clear visual hierarchy and guide the user's attention to important elements on the page.

Hick's Law

Organization/Company Name: Amazon

Website Address: https://www.amazon.com

Amazon's use of predictive search and filters help users quickly navigate through a large amount of content, reducing decision time and exemplifying Hick's Law.

PARC: Proximity

Organization/Company Name: Airbnb

Website Address: https://www.airbnb.com

Airbnb's use of proximity by grouping similar elements together and using white space to separate them creates a clear visual structure and helps users understand the information hierarchy.
